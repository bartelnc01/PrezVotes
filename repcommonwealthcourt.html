What does this court do?

This is one of the two intermediate appellate courts (alongside the Superior Court) in Pennsylvania. This 9-judge panel court focuses on actions done by the government and regulatory bodies such as with banking, utility regulation, taxation laws, elections, and labor. It also is where cases filed against the Pennsylvania State Government are heard. Appeals are usually heard only by 3 judges at a time. The president of the Commonwealth Court has a 5 year term and is elected by their colleagues.
